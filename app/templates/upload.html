Why We Need Your Reference Images

SeeSea uses AI to automatically identify you in surfing photos. To do this effectively, we need clear reference images of:

  • Your face - So we can recognize you in the water
  • Your surfboard - To help identify you when your face isn't visible
  • Wetsuit details - Additional information to improve matching accuracy

The better your reference images, the more accurately we can identify you in action shots!

Tips for Good Reference Images
Face Photos
  • Use good lighting - avoid shadows on your face
  • Look directly at the camera for front view
  • Remove sunglasses and hats if possible
  • Neutral expression works best
Surfboard Photos
  • Capture the entire board if possible
  • Highlight any distinctive designs or stickers
  • Take photos in good lighting
  • Include both top and bottom if they have different designs
